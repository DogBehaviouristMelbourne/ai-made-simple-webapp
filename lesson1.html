Day 1: AI Basics with Poe.com

Introduction to AI Conversations

Welcome to your first day of "AI Made Simple"! Today, you'll learn how to have meaningful conversations with AI using Poe.com, a free and user-friendly platform that gives you access to multiple AI assistants.

Poe.com allows you to:

  • Chat with different AI models without any technical setup
  • Ask questions and get detailed answers on almost any topic
  • Compare responses from different AI assistants
  • Save your conversations for future reference

Beginner Activity: Your First AI Conversation

Let's start with a simple activity to get you comfortable talking with AI:

  1. Visit Poe.com and create a free account
  2. Choose "Claude" or "ChatGPT" from the available AI assistants
  3. Type this question: "What are 3 ways AI can help someone with no technical background?"
  4. Read the response and ask a follow-up question about one of the suggestions

Tips for better conversations:

  • Be specific in your questions
  • Ask one question at a time
  • If the answer is too complex, ask the AI to explain it more simply

Bonus Task: Compare AI Assistants

Different AI assistants have different strengths. Try this comparison activity:

  1. Ask the same question to two different AI assistants on Poe (e.g., Claude and ChatGPT)
  2. Compare their answers - notice the differences in style, detail, and approach
  3. Try this question: "Explain artificial intelligence as if I'm 10 years old"

This exercise will help you understand which AI assistant you prefer for different types of questions.

Reflection Question

Take a moment to think about this question (you can even discuss it with the AI):

"What task in your daily life would you most like AI help with, and why?"

This reflection will help you identify practical applications of AI in your own life.
