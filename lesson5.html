Day 5: AI for Learning with HuggingFace Spaces

Introduction to AI for Continuous Learning

Welcome to the final day of "AI Made Simple"! Today, you'll explore HuggingFace Spaces, a platform that hosts thousands of interactive AI demos you can use to continue your learning journey.

HuggingFace Spaces allows you to:

  • Explore interactive AI demos without any technical setup
  • Try different types of AI models for various tasks
  • Learn about cutting-edge AI capabilities
  • Continue your AI education at your own pace

Beginner Activity: Explore AI Question Answering

Let's explore an AI that can answer questions based on specific text:

  1. Visit HuggingFace Spaces (no account needed)
  2. In the search bar, type "question answering" and press Enter
  3. Look for a demo called "Question Answering" or similar and click on it
  4. In the "Context" box, paste a paragraph of text (e.g., from a news article or Wikipedia)
  5. In the "Question" box, type a question about the text you pasted
  6. Click "Submit" and see how the AI finds the answer in your text

Tips for exploring HuggingFace Spaces:

  • Browse the "Featured Spaces" section for interesting demos
  • Use the search function to find specific types of AI (e.g., "image generation", "text summarization")
  • Read the descriptions to understand what each demo does

Bonus Task: Create a Learning Resource with AI

Let's use what you've learned to create a personalized learning resource:

  1. Think of a topic you're interested in learning more about
  2. Use Poe.com (from Lesson 1) to ask: "Create a beginner's guide to [your topic]"
  3. Save the response as a reference guide
  4. Use Craiyon.com (from Lesson 2) to create a visual representation of the topic
  5. Use Taskade (from Lesson 4) to create a learning plan for this topic

This exercise combines multiple AI tools you've learned about to create a comprehensive learning resource tailored to your interests.
